Open positions

Come work with us

We are looking for motivated M.S. (Research) and PhD students to tackle challenging questions about the role of the ocean in climate.

Who we're looking for

A background in fluid dynamics, thermodynamics, oceanography, atmospheric or climate science, physics, mathematics, or AI/ML — together with some coding experience in Python, MATLAB, NCL, or Ferret.

How to apply

Apply through the IIT Delhi PG admissions portal, and feel free to email Prof. Saurabh Rathore at srathore@iitd.ac.in if you're interested in working with the lab.
