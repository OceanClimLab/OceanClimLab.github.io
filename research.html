Research

What we work on

Six threads run through the lab. Each starts from a question about how the ocean is changing — and what that change means for the climate system.

01

Ocean Warming & Sea Level

Detecting and attributing global and regional ocean warming, and the sea-level rise it drives, across observations and models.

02

Ocean Circulation & Water Masses

Ocean circulation, the formation and transformation of water masses, and the mesoscale eddies that stir heat and carbon through the ocean.

03

Marine Heatwaves & Compound Extremes

Marine heatwaves and compound extreme events — their drivers, their evolution, and the impacts that cascade from them.

04

The Changing Water Cycle

How a warming climate reshapes the global and regional hydrological cycle, from ocean salinity fingerprints to rainfall on land.

05

Climate Modelling

Climate models are used to understand present-day change and to project where the system is heading.

06

Machine learning for Climate

Data-driven and machine-learning methods applied to oceanographic and climate problems, from pattern detection to emulation.
